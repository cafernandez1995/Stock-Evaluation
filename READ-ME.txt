The .csv files added here are copies of the .xlsx files of the same name.
Data analysis was run in RStudio and can be opened/run in either R or RStudio, both of which are free to download.
The R files can also be read using a text opening software (Notepad) and unserstood with the comments, which are lines beginning with an "#".
Summary statistics were manually exported into .txt files so they could be viewed without the use of R/RStudio.
